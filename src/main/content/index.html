Awesomesauce

An IBM Open Source Project

Create more, faster

Developing apps for container platforms requires harmony between developers, architects and operations. Enterprise developers today worry about much more than writing code. Architects and operations get overloaded with choices, standards, and compliance after the fact. Awesomesauce simplifies modernization of apps and speeds development of new cloud native applications built to meet technology standards and company policies you define. Develop, build, deploy, and manage with speed and control!

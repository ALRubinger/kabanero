Kabanero

Create more, faster

Developing apps for container platforms requires harmony between developers, architects, and operations. Today’s enterprise developers need to be efficient at much more than writing code. Architects and operations get overloaded with choices, standards, and compliance work later than anticipated. Kabanero simplifies the modernization of apps and speeds development of new cloud native applications built to meet technology standards and company policies you define. Develop, build, deploy, and manage with speed and control!

Developer Architect Operations

Develop more, faster.

Use your favorite development tools to create apps for Kubernetes faster. Develop, test, and debug in containers, and deploy with confidence knowing you’re automatically compliant with company policies. Spend more time writing code rather than re-tooling and building deep Kubernetes skills. Select a stack customized by your team for languages and programming models that you use on a daily basis.

100% Open Source. Automated. Integrated.

Design more, faster.

Use pre-defined Knapp Paks to provide proven industry patterns that you customize to match your business process and needs. Knapp Paks let developers focus on the business problem because the infrastructure, CI/CD, and deployment is taken care of as part of the Knapp Pak and platform definition. Using Knapp Paks you can focus on how you want to solve business problems, not infrastructure decisions, reducing decision cycles and time to value.

100% Open Source. Automated. Integrated.

Maintain more, faster.

Maintain compliance with confidence, using certified Cloud Paks to deliver the latest container images with the latest security patches. Bring your existing applications into your hybrid cloud infrastructure. You get immediate visibility of what your apps need to modernize – self-service tools show business value and build you a plan, offering a seamless lift-and-shift without recompiling your code. Kabanero is one platform for existing and new apps, with a single view across hybrid deployments, whether in containers or VMs.

100% Open Source. Automated. Integrated.
